Our Phased Development Roadmap

We will build Transerv through a strategic, three-phased approach, starting with a Minimum Viable Product for initial clients and scaling to a full-featured platform.

1

Phase 1: MVP & Custom Clients

Deliver core mobile data collection, offline sync, and secure cloud storage. Focus on custom reporting and hosting for our two initial clients to build case studies and secure early revenue.

2

Phase 2: General Use & Professional Tier

Launch tiered SaaS subscriptions. Introduce dynamic dashboards, visualization tools, and initial AI-powered species identification. Build community features for citizen scientists.

3

Phase 3: Advanced Analytics & Enterprise

Roll out advanced AI/ML models for behavioral analysis and predictive modeling. Implement deep GIS integration (Esri) and IoT/sensor data ingestion for enterprise-level clients.
